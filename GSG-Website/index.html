Welcome To The Gunslinger Gang

    • What Are Gunslingers?

    Gunglinger Gang (GSG) is an NFT project comprised of a Total 10,000 different gunslinging outlaws.



    Each gunslinger is composed of a Distinct set of attributes including their hat, bandana, beard, poncho, outfit and much more. Every gunslinger is a Unique collectible with proof of ownership stored on the Ethereum Blockchain.


    Mint Your Gunslinger

    Fair Distribution

    Every Gunslinger costs 0.01ETH and there is a total collection of 10,000. There are no price tiers, all GSG tokens cost the same for everyone. Each address is limited to minting no more than 3 tokens.
